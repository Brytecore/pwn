Pwn - a lightweight, responsive CSS framework

From the developers at Brytecore.

No bloat. More breakpoints. Any screen size.

Pwn was designed based on our need for a stable, consistent framework to use in our website themes and admin pages. After adding hundreds of styles to simply "remove" existing styles in large frameworks, we went looking for a smaller footprint in our base CSS. We found plenty of skeletal frameworks, but they had too "few" features (like not enough breakpoints for modern devices) or were too rigid.

Pwn is perfect for our needs, and we decided to share our work with you, via permissive open source licensing. Enjoy!
